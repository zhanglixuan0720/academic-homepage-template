Lixuan Zhang is the Ph.D. student in Visual Information Processing and Learning ([VIPL](http://vipl.ict.ac.cn/en/)) group from the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Prof. [Xilin Chen](http://vipl.ict.ac.cn/people/_xlchen/). He also have a close collaboration with Prof. [Meina Kan](https://vipl.ict.ac.cn/homepage/mnkan/index.html).
